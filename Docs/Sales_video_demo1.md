# MedAssist AI — 2-Minute Sales Demo Script
## Format: Voiceover + Screenshare | Audience: Business / Executive

---

> **DELIVERY NOTES:** ~240 words. Speak at a steady, confident pace — not rushed.
> Every [SCREEN] cue is a tab switch. Every [PAUSE] is a 1-second breath.
> This is a sales room, not a tech review — lead with feeling, close with proof.

Every conversation was heard. Every symptom captured. Every SOAP note drafted, coded, and waiting for their signature — in real time, while they were still in the room with their patient.

**That is MedAssist AI.**

[SCREEN: Doctor dashboard — clean, role-based view]

This is the physician dashboard. Patient queue, appointment status, medical records — all live, all role-secured.

[SCREEN: Voice documentation / chat screen — mic active]

When a consultation begins, passive listening activates. No button. No dictation. The doctor just talks to their patient.

[PAUSE]

[SCREEN: Transcript or structured output appearing on screen]

Azure AI is listening — separating voices, filtering small talk, extracting what matters clinically.

[SCREEN: Full SOAP note output — Subjective / Objective / Assessment / Plan visible]

And here — a complete clinical note. ICD-10 coded. Structured. Ready.

[PAUSE]

[SCREEN: One-click approval button or physician review screen]

The physician reviews, edits if needed, and approves.

[PAUSE]

[SCREEN: Confirmation / notification — record published]

Done. Note signed. EHR updated. Audio deleted — **permanently** — because we never store what we don't need to.

**Forty to sixty percent less time on documentation. More time with patients. Zero audio retained.**
